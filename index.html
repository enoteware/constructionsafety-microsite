Our Construction Safety Services

We provide comprehensive safety consulting services tailored to the unique requirements of federal, state, and private construction projects.

SSHO Services for Federal Construction

Site Safety and Health Officers (SSHO) for USACE, NAVFAC, and federal military construction projects. Full EM 385-1-1 compliance, Accident Prevention Plans, Activity Hazard Analyses, and on-site safety management for projects requiring federal safety standards.

Learn More →

Safety Representatives

Dedicated on-site safety professionals for construction projects of all types and sizes. Daily safety inspections, Cal-OSHA compliance verification, safety training coordination, incident investigation, and comprehensive safety program implementation.

Learn More →

Safety Program Development

Custom safety program creation including Injury and Illness Prevention Programs (IIPP), Job Hazard Analyses (JHA), safety manuals, emergency response plans, and contractor safety management programs tailored to your specific project requirements and regulatory obligations.

Safety Training & Consulting

OSHA safety training, toolbox talks, safety audits, compliance assessments, and expert safety consulting for construction companies seeking to improve their safety culture, reduce incidents, and maintain regulatory compliance.

Why Construction Companies Choose Us

Our certifications, credentials, and experience set us apart in the construction safety consulting industry.

SDVOSB & DVBE Certified

We're certified as both a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) and Disabled Veteran Business Enterprise (DVBE). Our veteran-owned leadership (USMC & U.S. Navy) understands military culture and helps federal contractors meet small business subcontracting goals on USACE, NAVFAC, and other government projects. Our DVBE certification provides Caltrans contractors with a 5% bid advantage on state-funded projects.

Highly Credentialed Team

Our safety professionals hold the industry's highest certifications including Certified Industrial Hygienist (CIH), Certified Safety Professional (CSP), Construction Health and Safety Technician (CHST), and 30-Hour OSHA Construction Safety certification. We're not entry-level safety technicians—we're experienced professionals with advanced degrees and decades of construction safety expertise.

Federal & California Expertise

We specialize in both federal safety requirements (EM 385-1-1, USACE, NAVFAC) and California-specific regulations (Cal-OSHA Title 8, Caltrans Standard Specifications). Based in San Diego with extensive experience throughout California and nationwide, we understand the unique compliance challenges of construction projects in both federal and state jurisdictions.

About Construction Safety Consulting

Construction Safety Consulting is a division of EHS Analytical Solutions, Inc., a leading environmental health and safety consulting company based in San Diego, California. Founded by service-disabled veterans with backgrounds in the U.S. Marine Corps and U.S. Navy, our company brings military discipline, attention to detail, and unwavering commitment to safety excellence.

Our mission is to protect construction workers and ensure regulatory compliance through expert safety services, professional guidance, and proactive hazard management. We partner with general contractors, construction managers, and project owners to create safer jobsites, reduce incidents, and maintain compliance with OSHA, Cal-OSHA, EM 385-1-1, and all applicable safety regulations.

As a certified SDVOSB and DVBE, we help contractors meet their small business participation goals while delivering the highest quality safety services in the industry. Our team's combination of advanced credentials, real-world construction experience, and commitment to continuous improvement makes us the trusted safety partner for construction projects throughout California and nationwide.
